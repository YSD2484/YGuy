Hazards Here is a list of common hazards while using the internet:  
Identity theft

……. this is when a criminal collect you personal information e.g. bank details and uses it for crime.
Spyware

…….. is a computer program that has the ability to 'spy'on your computer by saving the websites opened, the key strokes entered or downloads, then it sends it to another source where someone can use it for fraud or marketing.
Hackers …..… are people who attempt to hack/ access your private files without authority, they have malicious purpose. these people are very dangerous
Viruses …….. are programs that are able to infect your computer (without permission) and cause random pop-ups and ruin the programs in the computer by corrupting them. this is very dangerous
Cookies ……... is a small file which stores information related to your use of the Internet and reports it back to the service provider. They can cause pop-ups and they store passwords
Spam …..… is unwanted and unnecessary emails or other media sent for mostly advertisment, earning money per clicks and spreading viruses
Phishing …….. involves sending a link via an email to a web site which is not genuine (bogus). You may be tricked into entering personal information. Its aim is to steal credit card details, usernames and passwords.
